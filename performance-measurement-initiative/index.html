4 Program Evaluation Workshops

Several years ago, one of our major activities was to conduct a series of several day impact assessment workshops that teach members of non-profits and socially conscious businesses how to conduct outcome evaluations. We conducted workshops in Ann Arbor, New York City, and Johannesburg, South Africa.
