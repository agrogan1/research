Country Level Bans on Corporal Punishment

Author

Andy Grogan-Kaylor

Published

April 5, 2025

1 Background

68 countries have instituted country wide bans upon the use of corporal punishment with children. The most recent country to institute a ban is Thailand.

Information on countries that have banned corporal punishment available from endcorporalpunishment.org
